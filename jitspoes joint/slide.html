     Intro
No... I didn't make SLIDE... or even work on it... I didn't make any levels for it either (although I might).  I did, however, make some record times on some of the levels :).  What's SLIDE?  It's basically a Quake1 mod where you jump on a hover board and cruse down tracks with no top speed.  You can also jump off your board, grab a rocket launcher, and blow other sliders up. :)  Wanna get it?  Check out The S-L-I-D-E Home Page.  The reason I put this section on my page is because I feel this is a really fun mod that lacks recognition (maybe it doesn't, in which case I've just got this here because it's a cool mod :).
